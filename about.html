About STAR

STAR (Scrub Typhus Awareness & Risk Prediction) is a platform designed to raise awareness, support early detection, and assist in tracking Scrub Typhus cases. Our goal is to empower communities, individuals, and healthcare workers with accurate insights to reduce the spread of Scrub Typhus.

Scrub Typhus is caused by the Orientia tsutsugamushi bacteria and is transmitted through bites from infected chiggers. Early diagnosis and awareness are essential, and STAR provides tools that make this easier.

Key Features of STAR:

  • Nearby Case Tracker: Locate nearby Scrub Typhus hotspots using interactive maps.
  • Self Assessment: Instantly evaluate your risk level based on your symptoms.
  • Prediction System: AI-based outbreak forecasting using climate and past case data.
  • Diagnostic Centers: Quickly find nearby hospitals and test centers.

STAR is fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop use. Our mission is to combine technology with healthcare to fight Scrub Typhus effectively and globally.
